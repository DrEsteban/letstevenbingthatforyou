About Let me Bing that for you!

Despite what our well intentioned teachers told us in elementary school, there is such a thing as a stupid question. And stupid questions deserve snarky answers.

Let me Bing that for you is for all those people that love to bother you with their question rather than simply Bing it themselves. While answering such questions in a funny manner, you are also educating those people on how to use search. Teach a man to fish, as they say.

  • Development: Maarten Balliauw, Juliën Hanssens, Phil Haack, Mateus de Morais
  • Inspiration: Let me Google that for you
  • Contact: Mateus de Morais
